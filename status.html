📱 Onboarding Redesign (9 Cards, Emotional-First)

Why Emotional-First?

Inspired by Arise case study: 13% → 23% conversion by designing for emotional recognition, not features. Our onboarding targets actual parent pain points.

The 9-Card Journey

CARD 1: Recognition
"You've Tried Everything. They Still Eat 5 Foods."

Parent sees their struggle. Validates feelings. Removes guilt.

CARD 2: Validation
"Stop Blaming Yourself" — It's neurological, not behavioral

Explains SOS method (LOOK→TOUCH→SMELL→LICK→TASTE). Gives permission to stop guilt.

CARD 3: Proof
"Real Parents. Real Progress." — Testimonials from Portland families

Social proof. Shows others faced exact same struggle. Hope.

CARD 4: Partnership
"We Do This Together" — You log. We show patterns. They get braver.

Reframes badges/rewards as earned confidence, not bribes.

CARDS 5-9: Action
Explorer selection → Safe foods collection → Sensory profile → Goal food → First log

Progressive data collection, personal investment.

Key Insight

Card+Modal Pattern: Headlines + images on card (scannable, quick), full copy in modal (optional depth). Respects different learning styles, solves space problem.

🛡️ LLM Guardrails (10 Validation Rules)

Prevent nonsensical suggestions (e.g., "salty liquid as a meal"). Hard rules before LLM generates.

The 10 Rules

  1. Never suggest liquid foods as primary (except beverages)
  2. Avoid bitter flavors entirely (safety for sensitive kids)
  3. Don't combine 3+ bold flavors simultaneously
  4. Validate temperature + texture combos
  5. ONLY suggest foods in 100+ database
  6. Enforce color variety (prevent monochrome diets)
  7. Require food group diversity
  8. Bridge foods must share ≥1 sensory attribute with safe foods
  9. Sensory distance ≤1 step (texture & flavor)
  10. Weekly batch processing only (not real-time spam)

Forbidden Combos Blocked

  • Salty + Liquid = broth, salted water
  • Bitter + Any = entirely avoided
  • Hot + Crunchy = sensory conflict
  • 3+ Bold Flavors = overwhelming
  • Mushy + Sweet = potential gag trigger
