Architecture

  • 8 Planets: Base Camp, Sensory Grove, Texture Trails, Aroma Airship, Flavor Mountains, Taste Ocean, Taste Jungle, Galaxy's Heart
  • 4 Explorers: Nova (Speed), Cosmo (Science), Star (Brave), Orbit (Adventurous)

Food Database

  • Coverage: 100+ foods across 12 categories
  • Sensory Attributes Tracked: Texture (crunchy, soft, chewy, liquid, mushy, mixed), Flavor (sweet, salty, savory, sour, bitter, bland, spicy), Temperature (cold, room temp, warm, hot), Color (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, brown, white, mixed), Food Group
  • LLM Guardrails: 10 validation rules + forbidden combos

Gamification

  • 20 Badges: Unlock conditions + rarity tiers
  • Points Economy: 10+ action types, phase-based planet progression
  • Parent Dashboard: Pattern analysis, food group distribution, sensory attributes, bridge food recommendations, trend graphs

AI Features

  • Bridge Food Matching: Analyzes logged foods → sensory distance calculation → personalized recommendations
  • Sensory Pattern Recognition: Texture preferences, flavor tolerances, temperature sensitivity, color aversion
  • Risk Indicators: Confidence levels on recommendations before presenting to parent
