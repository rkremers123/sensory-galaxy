Why AI UGC Personas for Sensory Galaxy?

UGC = User-Generated Content. But what if the "user" is an AI avatar—a consistent, on-brand character who can create unlimited TikTok/Reels content in minutes?

The Case for an AI Mom Persona

  • Consistency: Same person, same voice, same vibe across all videos. No booking different creators.
  • Speed: Generate 10 TikToks in a day. No shooting, no editing, no back-and-forth with creators.
  • Cost: $100-500/month vs. $1,000-5,000/month for real influencers.
  • Control: 100% brand alignment. The avatar says exactly what you want, how you want it.
  • Authenticity: If done well, viewers see "relatable mom" not "corporate bot." Emotional connection works.
  • Scalability: One persona, infinite content. Test messaging fast, iterate faster.

The Sensory Galaxy Mom Persona Concept

Meet: "Sarah" (or your name of choice)

  • Age: 35-42
  • Bio: Mom of two (7-year-old with sensory sensitivity, 4-year-old). Works part-time. Portland-based (relatability).
  • Vibe: Warm, honest, a bit tired-but-hopeful. Not polished. Real.
  • Content Style: Phone selfies (TikTok style), talking directly to camera. "Hey, I just tried this with my kid..." No fancy production.
  • Message: "This saved our mealtimes" / "My kid actually asked for broccoli" / "The guilt is finally gone"

📋 Step-by-Step: Create Your AI Mom Persona

Phase 1: Define Your Persona (30 min)

1 Name & Bio

Pick a name (Sarah, Jessica, Amanda, whatever feels right). Write a one-liner bio:

"Sarah, 38, mom of two. Gave up forcing mealtimes. Now we just... explore together. Life's way better."

2 Visual Design

Decide on appearance:

  • Age: 35-42
  • Hair: Natural colors (blonde, brunette, redhead)
  • Style: Casual mom (t-shirt, hoodie, light sweater—nothing fancy)
  • Background: Home setting (kitchen, living room, couch)
  • Vibe: Warm, tired but hopeful, relatable
3 Voice & Tone

Script her voice personality:

  • Speed: Normal, not rushed. Like texting a friend.
  • Filler words: Okay to use "uh," "like," "so" (makes it real)
  • Emotion: Conversational. Not scripted. Sometimes laughs.
  • Accent: Optional. Regional accent (slight) can add authenticity.
4 Content Themes

3-5 core topics she talks about:

  • "My kid finally tried something new"
  • "We stopped fighting at dinner"
  • "This approach actually works"
  • "I'm not the only one struggling"
  • "Here's what changed for us"

Phase 2: Set Up Your Tool (1-2 hours)

1 Create Account

Sign up on MakeUGC (or your chosen tool). Connect your email, brand info, payment method.

2 Upload Avatar Assets

Depending on tool, you either:

  • Option A (MakeUGC): Use pre-built avatars from their library. Customize clothing/hair/background.
  • Option B (D-ID): Upload a photo of the person you want to represent (real person or stock photo). AI animates them.

⚠️ Legal Note: If using a real person's photo, get written permission. AI personas should be clearly disclosed.

3 Set Up Script Templates

Create 5-10 script templates your avatar will use:

TEMPLATE 1: "The Breakthrough"
[Sarah looks at camera, slight smile]
"So... my daughter tried broccoli yesterday.
Not because I made her.
Because she was actually curious.
And I was standing there in the kitchen like... did this actually happen?"
[Laughs]
"Download Sensory Galaxy. It changed everything for us."

TEMPLATE 2: "The Relief"
"Can I be honest? I thought I was doing something wrong.
My kid eats five foods. Five.
Then I realized... it's not picky.
It's sensory.
And there's literally a method for this.
Try it free. Seven days. No credit card."
4 Configure Voice & Accent

Most tools let you pick:

  • Voice: Female (age ~35-40)
  • Accent: American English (or regional if you want specificity)
  • Pace: Normal (100%)
  • Tone: Warm, conversational

Phase 3: Generate Your First Videos (2-3 hours)

1 Write 10 Scripts

Use your templates. Vary the message. Each script = 15-30 seconds of video.

Example themes:

  • "I felt like a failure before"
  • "Nobody tells you this can be neurological"
  • "Seven days changed everything"
  • "My therapist recommended this"
  • "Here's what actually helped"
2 Generate Videos in Batch

In MakeUGC (or your tool):

  • Paste script into text field
  • Select avatar + background + voice
  • Hit "Generate" → Wait 2-5 minutes
  • Download .mp4 (1080p vertical for TikTok)
3 Edit & Add Captions

Most tools auto-caption. But you can enhance:

  • Add text overlay: "Try free →"
  • Add hook at top (first 3 seconds): "This saved our mealtimes"
  • Add CTA at end: "Link in bio for 7-day free trial"
  • Add trending music (optional, TikTok-friendly)
4 Upload to TikTok

Post 2-3 videos/week from your brand account (@sensorygalaxyapp).

✅ Pro tip: Most tools have "auto-upload" integrations. Connect TikTok directly + schedule posts in bulk.

Phase 4: Iterate & Optimize (Ongoing)

1 Track Metrics

After 10-20 videos, analyze:

  • Which scripts get most views?
  • Which get most saves/shares?
  • Which drive signups to the 7-day trial?
2 Double Down on Winners

Generate more videos using similar scripts/hooks/messages. TikTok algorithm rewards consistency.

3 Test New Hooks

Keep trying new angles:

  • "Things nobody told me about selective eating"
  • "My therapist said this..."
  • "7 days ago my kid refused everything"

⚠️ Ethical Concerns & FTC Disclosure

BE TRANSPARENT: You MUST disclose that your persona is AI-generated. The FTC and TikTok both require clear disclosure of synthetic content.

How to Disclose

  • In bio: Add to your @sensorygalaxyapp TikTok bio: "🤖 AI-powered brand storyteller. Sharing real parent experiences through digital avatar."
  • In video caption: Add #AIGenerated or #SyntheticMedia tag to every video
  • In pinned comment: "This character is an AI avatar created to share authentic parent experiences about sensory eating."
  • In FAQ: Address it directly on your website FAQ: "Our brand ambassador is an AI avatar designed to represent real parent perspectives."

Why Transparency Works

Paradoxically, disclosing AI actually builds trust. Modern audiences understand AI. They just want honesty. If you hide it, discovery = scandal. If you're upfront, it's just... a marketing tool.

✅ Best Practice: Combine your AI persona with real testimonials from actual users. AI avatar shares parent perspective + real parents confirm authenticity. Best of both worlds.
