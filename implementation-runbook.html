Phase 2: Email & Social Setup

Task 1: Google Workspace Email (5-10 min)

Goal: Set up hello@sensorygalaxy.com for customer support, beta signups, partnerships.

1 Go to google.com/workspace
2 Click "Get Started" → Select Business Starter ($6/user/month)
3 Enter domain: sensorygalaxy.com
4 Verify domain ownership:
  • Vercel already has your DNS → should auto-verify
  • If manual needed: Add TXT record from Google to your Vercel DNS
5 Create email: hello@sensorygalaxy.com (or whatever address makes sense)
6 Set up forwarding (optional):
  • Forward to your personal email for now
  • Later: Use Google Workspace email directly
⏱️ Time estimate: 5-10 minutes. Mostly waiting for domain verification.

Task 2: Instagram Handle Reservation (2 min)

Goal: Lock in @sensorygalaxyapp on Instagram before anyone else grabs it.

1 Go to instagram.com → Sign in (or create account)

  • Name: Sensory Galaxy
  • Bio: "Sensory feeding intelligence. Coming soon." (or empty for now)

Phase 3: Site Rebuild (FAQ, Sign-Up, Pricing)

Overview

Add 3 new pages to sensory-galaxy website. Keep design consistent with landing page (dark starfield, cyan/gold accents, emotional messaging).

Page 1: FAQ Page

Purpose: Answer parent questions about SOS method, app features, pricing, data privacy.

FAQ Structure

  • About Selective Eating: What is sensory sensitivity? Why won't forcing help? How long does it take?
  • About SOS Method: What are the 5 phases? Do I have to follow them in order? What if my kid resists?
  • About the App: What data does it collect? Is it secure? Can I export data for my pediatrician?
  • Pricing & Trial: How does the 7-day trial work? No credit card? What happens after?
  • Support: How do I contact you? Do you have therapist partnerships?
✍️ Copy Strategy: Answer like a therapist would. Warm, knowledgeable, not clinical. Use examples from real parents.

Page 2: Sign-Up Page

Purpose: Get parents into 7-day free trial. No credit card. Just email.

Sign-Up Flow

1 Hero Section: "Start Your Free 7-Day Trial"
  • Copy: "No credit card. No commitment. See if Sensory Galaxy works for your family."
  • Visual: Hero image (parent + child, warm lighting, hopeful)
2 Form:
  • Child's name
  • Child's age
  • Parent email
  • [CTA Button]: "Start Free Trial"
3 Trust Signals (below form):
  • ✅ No credit card required
  • ✅ Full access to all features (7 days)
  • ✅ Cancel anytime
  • ✅ Your data is secure
4 Timeline (optional, below form):
  • Day 1-2: Onboarding (9 cards, data collection)
  • Day 3-5: Start logging foods, exploring planets
  • Day 6: Review progress, see patterns
  • Day 7: Decide: Continue ($3.99/month) or cancel
