How It Works

Three things the app does to help your family

1️⃣ Understand the Pattern

You log what your child explores. The app finds patterns: which textures do they gravitate toward? What flavors are they comfortable with? Are they progressing, or stuck? What should you try next?

Parent benefit: Removes guesswork. Real proof they're getting braver.

2️⃣ Get Smart Suggestions

After 3-5 foods logged, our AI recommends bridge foods—foods that match what they like, but introduce something new. If they like crunchy + salty crackers, try crunchy + salty pretzels. Every suggestion is doable.

Parent benefit: Less rejection. More progress.

3️⃣ Celebrate Progress

Your child earns streaks, badges, and cosmetics for exploring. You see the data. Mealtimes shift from battles to "look how brave they're being." The guilt? It's gone.

Parent benefit: Hope. Proof. Celebration.
